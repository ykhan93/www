BRAND STORY

WORLD NO.1
COFFEE & DONUTS
Dunkin' Donuts is the world's largest coffee & donut specialty
brand with a 70-year history, operating 11,300 stores in 26
countries around the world.
Dunkin' Donuts Korea has grown every year since opening its first
store in Itaewon in 1994, establishing itself as the No. 1 brand
boasting unrivaled market share.
As of 2022, you can enjoy a sweet break with various products such as sweet donuts,
fragrant coffee, and differentiated beverages at 770 Dunkin Donuts stores nationwide.
