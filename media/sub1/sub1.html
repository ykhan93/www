STORY

Peter, whose daily life is messed up because of Mysterio, and even Ned and MJ, whom he has brought in, are frustrated with his admission to MIT.

Peter asks Doctor Strange to change the past, but he refuses. Instead, others decide to erase the memory that Peter Parker was Spider-Man. However, Peter interrupts the spell by asking for exceptions to those who knew his identity before the revelation, and Strange's magic fails.

As a result, characters from a parallel universe who knew Spider-Man's true identity came.

With the help of Ned and MJ, he meets Sandman from Spider-Man 3 and Electro from The Amazing Spider-Man 2 and captures both. After a brief break from the evil Green Goblin's personality, Norman Osbourne goes to May's workplace, and goes with Peter to the basement of Sanctum. Doctor Strange tries to bring them back, but to protect Spider-Man's doomed foes, Peter and Strange fight over a magic box. Peter, who succeeded in isolating Strange in the Mirror Dimension, conducts research to free the villains, solve their physical/mental abnormalities, and return them, and free Doctor Octopus from the control of the Mechanical Arm.
